RESEARCHATHON 2.0
Witness the genesis of ingenious ideas at this one-of-a-kind competitive research event, where you get to put your problem solving skills to a rigorous test, thinking up fresh and innovative solutions, culminating with a presentation in front of few of the brilliant minds in research.
DESCRIPTION


Physics is a quest for truth, a search for the understanding of the underlying workings of the universe. This event Researchathon intends to take up this quest, add spices to it and serve the finest quality food to your brains in the form of competitive research.

5 teams selected based on rigorous prelims which will include physics quizzes and other qualifiers, will be given a choice to choose their fields of interest and a problem from that field will be given to work on. After working on the problem, a presentation is supposed to be delivered in front of a panel of judges who will evaluate your work based on various criteria.

The entire event would be conducted online, so you do not have to worry about travel and other hassles.
